research

Isotope Geochemistry

2.5 billion years ago, the evolution of oxygen-producing photosynthesis flooded the atmosphere with oxygen. This event affected atmospheric reactions which left geologic sulfur signatures.

DNA Data Storage

Just as digital storage uses binary (0/1) to encode information, we can also store data in DNA bases (A/T/C/G). While read/write speeds are slow, DNA is both very dense and highly stable.

Experimental Evolution

The process of evolution can be recorded across generations using modern deep-sequencing techniques. This process is performed with microbes such as E. coli and yeast for fast reproductive periods.

Savannah Ecology

A mutualism between acacia trees and ants flourishes in East Africa. The tree provides a home from a modified stipule structure and food from extrafloral nectaries, while the ants defend the tree from attackers.
